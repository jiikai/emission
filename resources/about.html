About this app

The Emission app is built around the emiss API, developed with the ultimate aim of providing a general, lightweight interface in C for extracting, storing, and serving data by the World Bank (and perhaps other parties).

More chart types, filtering options, etc. may be added to this app in the future. Maybe a more fetching stylesheet as well. However, this developer's focus may well be elsewhere, in the backend of the API or otherwise.

The app is subject to this license. Thanks for visiting & have a nice day!
